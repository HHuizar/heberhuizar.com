Profile

Biologist & Environmental
Management Specialist

I work at the intersection of marine mammal protection, geophysical survey operations, and environmental compliance. My offshore career spans Tiers 1 through 3 under NMFS guidelines, with real-time mitigation responsibilities across seismic, AUV, and multibeam survey programs.

In parallel, I hold a Master's in International Environmental Management from El Colegio de la Frontera Norte (COLEF), where my research centered on urban ecology, environmental justice, and GIS-based spatial analysis of public green infrastructure.

I work as an independent contractor, bridging operational field expertise with peer-reviewed academic output. My approach to both roles is the same: methodical, skeptical of assumptions, and oriented toward documentation and evidence.
